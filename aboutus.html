Who Are We?

Code of the Future was founded by me, Ellie Sleigtholm! I am a third-year mathematics student and YouTuber who has recently finished an internship on the Space Placements in Industry Scheme managed by the UK Space Agency! I absolutely love to code and I have a huge passion for everything tech! Coding and helping students learn and enhance their skills are two of my favourite past-times. Through a series of YouTube videos, I aim to teach and provide high quality tutorials whilst also enhancing my own programming skills. Everything is completely free!

Encourage. Educate. Inspire.
